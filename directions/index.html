Great Taste of the Midwest

Festival Location and Directions

The Great Taste of the Midwest is held each year in Madison's Olin-Turville Park. The park overlooks beautiful lake Monona with a wonderful view of downtown Madison. Please click on the blue marker in the map to get directions to the festival grounds.
